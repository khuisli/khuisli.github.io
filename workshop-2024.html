# About

The 2024 Research Workshop on Fragments is hosted by the Institute for the Study of Language and Information (KHU-ISLI) at Kyung Hee University in Seoul, Korea. The first part of this workshop took place in June 2024 in Vigo, Spain, hosted by the Language Variation and Textual Categorization group at the University of Vigo (UVigo-LVTC). The second part will be held on September 6-7, 2024, in conjunction with the Joint Workshop on Linguistics and Language Processing (JWLLP). This research seminar is held in partnership with the Department of Language and Information at Pusan National University and the Linguistics Convergence Institute at Chosun University. Especially, the workshop will focus on theoretical and formal aspects of natural language analysis, including syntax, semantics, pragmatics, corpus linguistics, sign language linguistics, and their interfaces.

This year, we will hold two workshop sessions: the first in Vigo, Spain, and the second in Seoul, Korea.

Vigo Workshop: June 22nd (Sat), 2024, 09:00-15:00, at University of Vigo, Spain ➢ Check out Fragments2024 webpage by LVTC Research Group

Seoul Workshop: September 6th (Fri) - 7th (Sat), 2024, at Kyung Hee University, Seoul, Korea

We look forward to productive and inspiring sessions.

Organized by Institute for the Study of Language and Information (ISLI), Kyung Hee University
