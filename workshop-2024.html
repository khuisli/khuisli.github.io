# About

We are excited to announce the 2024 Joint Workshops on Fragments, a collaborative event hosted by Kyung Hee University's Institute for the Study of Language and Information (KHU-ISLI), the Language Variation and Textual Categorization group at the University of Vigo (UVigo-LVTC), and the Joint Workshop on Linguistics and Language Processing (JWLLP).

This year, we will hold two workshop sessions: the first in Vigo, Spain, and the second in Seoul, Korea.

We look forward to productive and inspiring sessions.

Organized by Institute for the Study of Language and Information (ISLI), Kyung Hee University
