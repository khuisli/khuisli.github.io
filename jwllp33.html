# JWLLP 33

The Institute for the Study of Language and Information (KH-ISLI) at Kyung Hee University is proud to host the "33rd Joint Workshops on Linguistics and Language Processing," held in Seoul, Korea, on Saturday, April 18th, Friday, 2025. The theme for this workshop is "(Mis)matches between Form and Function in Natural Languages," focusing on innovative approaches and methodologies in language response systems. Participants from diverse fields such as syntax, semantics, pragmatics, corpus linguistics, and cognitive science are invited to present their research and engage in interdisciplinary discussions. As a significant event in the academic calendar of KH-ISLI, the workshop continues to foster collaboration and advance research in language studies.

  • Theme: (Mis)matches between Form and Function in Natural Languages
  • Tentative Date(s): April 18th (Fri), 2025
  • Venue: College of Humanities building, Kyung Hee University, Seoul

    5. Follow the campus signs to reach the College of Humanities.

    1. Board a Line 1 subway train heading towards Hoegi Station.
